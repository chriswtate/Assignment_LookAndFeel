Top 5 of All Time

Movies

1. The Shawshank Redemption

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

Year: 1994
Director: Frank Darabont
Writers: Stephen King and Frank Darabont
Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman and Bob Gunton

2. The Godfather

The aging patriarch of an organized crime dynasty in postwar New York City transfers control of his clandestine empire to his reluctant youngest son.

Year: 1972
Director: Francis Ford Coppola
Writers: Mario Puzo and Francis Ford Coppola
Actors: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino and James Caan

3. The Dark Knight

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Year: 2008
Director: Christopher Nolan
Writers: Jonathan Nolan, Christopher Nolan and David S. Goyer
Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger and Aaron Eckhart

4. The Godfather: Part II

The early life and career of Vito Corleone in 1920s New York City is portrayed, while his son, Michael, expands and tightens his grip on the family crime syndicate.

Year: 1974
Director: Francis Ford Coppola
Writers: Mario Puzo and Francis Ford Coppola
Actors: Al Pacino, Robert De Niro and Robert Duvall

5. 12 Angry Men

The jury in a New York City murder trial is frustrated by a single member whose skeptical caution forces them to more carefully consider the evidence before jumping to a hasty verdict.

Year: 1957
Director: Signey Lumet
Writer: Reginal Rose
Actors: Henry Fonda, Lee J. Cobb and Martin Balsam

TV Shows

1. Breaking Bad

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future.

Years: 2008 - 2013
Creator: Vince Gilligan
Stars: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul and Anna Gunn

2. Band Of Brothers

The story of Easy Company of the U.S. Army 101st Airborne Division and their mission in World War II Europe, from Operation Overlord to V-J Day.

Year: 2001
Creators: Steven Spielberg and Tom Hanks
Stars: Scott Grimes, Damian Lewis and Ron Livingston

3. Chernobyl

In April 1986, an explosion at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics becomes one of the world's worst man-made catastrophes.

Year: 2019
Creator: Craig Mazin
Stars: Jessie Buckley, Jared Harris and Stellan Skarsgård

4. The Wire

The Baltimore drug scene, as seen through the eyes of drug dealers and law enforcement.

Years: 2002 - 2008
Creator: David Simon
Stars: Dominic West, Lance Reddick and Sonja Sohn

5. Avatar: The Last Airbender

Tn a war-torn world of elemental magic, a young boy reawakens to undertake a dangerous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar, and bring peace to the world.

Years: 2005 - 2008
Creators: Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko
Stars: Dee Bradley Baker, Zach Tyler Eisen and Mae Whitman
